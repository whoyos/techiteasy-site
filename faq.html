Yes — we support Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS devices.

Diagnostics & Devices

What is included in a diagnostic?

We check performance, errors, malware, storage health, and system stability. You receive a full report with recommended fixes.

Can you fix my device remotely?

Many issues can be fixed remotely. If physical repair is needed, we will tell you exactly what is required.

Do you work with older devices?

Yes — we support older laptops, desktops, and mobile devices.

Absolutely. We use encrypted tools and never store your data.
